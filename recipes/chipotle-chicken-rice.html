Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • onion
  • seasonings: chipotle, cumin, garlic, onion, salt
  • lime juice
  • long-grain white rice
  • oven-roasted diced tomatoes
  • chicken broth

Steps:

  1. season chicken
  2. brown chicken in pan
  3. chop onion
  4. saute onion
  5. add tomatoes, broth, rice, and chicken
  6. simmer 15 minutes
