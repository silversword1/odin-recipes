Rasta Pasta

Description:

Rasta pasta is creamy pasta tossed with Caribbean style jerk chicken and sauteed bell peppers. This dish is so full of flavor, and makes a great weeknight dinner that can be made in just half an hour! Itâ€™s creamy, rich, spicy, and cheesy.

Ingredients:

  • chicken
  • bell peppers
  • pasta
  • green onions
  • jerk seasoning
  • heavy cream
  • parmesan cheese
  • garlic

Steps:

  1. cook the pasta
  2. season chicken with jerk seasoning
  3. brown chicken in pan
  4. slice bell peppers
  5. mince garlic
  6. saute peppers and garlic
  7. meanwhile, cut chicken into pieces
  8. add heavy cream to pan of peppers and garlic
  9. simmer until thickened
  10. add parmesan and stir until melted
  11. add chicken and pasta into pan and toss
